Is this a page dedicated to just my dog? Yes, yes it is.

Q&A

Why is she named Kitty? I was initially a cat person who had wanted a pet cat so we settled by naming her Kitty.
Is she as cute as she looks? Yes, but don't let that fool you. She will attempt to murder any other animal that comes near her and can get very loud.
Does she have siblings? Yes, 1 brother named Tomi or Tô Mì (Vietnamese for noodle bowl) who lives with my uncle and his family.
Born September 25, 2020, Kitty is a chihuahua-terrier mix that my family got from my uncle's neighbors for free.
Kitty loves to lie down like a tipped-over cow or elongate herself horizontally on our beds.
She loves her purple mouse toy (and the color purple overall) and can trick you into thinking she's cute.
In conclusion, Kitty is a very good girl.
